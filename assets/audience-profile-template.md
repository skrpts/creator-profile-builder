## Reader Values and Motivations

- **What draws them:** _e.g. actionable insight, contrarian takes, deep technical detail_
- **What keeps them:** _e.g. consistent quality, genuine expertise, honest about trade-offs_
- **What makes them share:** _e.g. "I've been saying this for years", practical frameworks, surprising data_
- **Trust signals:** _what makes them believe this creator — experience, evidence, specificity_

## Reader Pain Points

- **Primary problems addressed:** _the 3-5 core problems this content helps with_
- **Urgency level:** _chronic frustration vs acute crisis vs curiosity_
- **Current alternatives:** _where they go if not here — competitors, communities, documentation_
- **Unmet needs:** _gaps in the content that the audience likely wants addressed_

## Repellents

What drives this audience away. These are hard boundaries — crossing them loses readers permanently.

- **Content repellents:** _e.g. fluff, padding, "in today's fast-paced world", obvious advice_
- **Tone repellents:** _e.g. condescension, forced enthusiasm, corporate speak_
- **Credibility repellents:** _e.g. AI-sounding prose, unsupported claims, humble-bragging_
- **Format repellents:** _e.g. clickbait headlines, excessive CTAs, gated content_

## Tone Calibration Map

How to calibrate tone for this specific audience.

- **Formality spectrum:** _where on the scale from academic to casual — with examples_
- **Humour tolerance:** _none, occasional, frequent — what type works_
- **Directness preference:** _blunt? diplomatic? provocative?_
- **Jargon tolerance:** _use freely? define once? avoid entirely?_
- **Evidence expectations:** _anecdotal OK? needs data? needs citations?_
- **Vulnerability tolerance:** _personal stories welcome? keep it professional? depends on context?_

## Content Preferences

Inferred from the creator's existing content patterns and what resonates.

- **Preferred formats:** _long-form essays, tutorials, opinion pieces, case studies_
- **Ideal length:** _word count range that matches audience attention_
- **Depth expectations:** _overview, working knowledge, deep-dive, exhaustive_
- **Frequency expectations:** _daily, weekly, monthly — based on content type_
- **Visual expectations:** _text-only, diagrams, code blocks, screenshots_

## Engagement Patterns

Where and how this audience interacts with the content.

- **Discovery channels:** _search, social, newsletter, word-of-mouth, aggregators_
- **Consumption mode:** _read in full, skim then deep-read, save for later_
- **Sharing behaviour:** _what triggers a share — format, platform, context_
- **Feedback patterns:** _comments, replies, DMs, quotes — what they respond to_
